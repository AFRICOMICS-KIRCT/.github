# Welcome to AfricOmics
## Kano Independent Research Centre Trust (KIRCT) Genetics & Bioinformatics Training Program

Welcome to the official GitHub organization for **AfricOmics**, in partnership with the **Kano Independent Research Centre Trust (KIRCT)**.  
This space is dedicated to hands-on training, collaborative research, and bioinformatics pipelines.

---

## About the KIRCT Training Program
The **Kano Independent Research Centre Trust (KIRCT)** training program is designed to provide:
- Practical experience in **genetics & genomics analysis**
- Hands-on exposure to **bioinformatics pipelines**
- Real-world research datasets and collaborative projects

---

## What You Will Find in This Organization
This GitHub organization will host:
- Training and hands-on materials & lecture notes  
- Genetics & genomics analysis pipelines  
- Variant calling and NGS workflows  
- Practice datasets  
- Assignments and project repositories  
- Documentation and standard operating procedures (SOPs)

---

## Expectations From Trainees
As a participant, you are expected to:
- Actively contribute to repositories during the period of training and even after conclusion.
- Follow **version control best practices**
- Maintain **clean, reproducible code**
- Respect **research ethics and data privacy**
- Collaborate professionally with teammates

---

## Basic Requirements
You should be familiar (or be ready to learn) with:
- Linux command line
- Git and GitHub workflows
- Python or R
- Basic genetics and molecular biology concepts

---

## Code of Conduct
We are committed to providing a **safe, respectful, and inclusive** learning environment.  
Any form of harassment, discrimination, or misconduct will not be tolerated.
